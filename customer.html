Achievements

In Solar System Business:

Led a group about 30 staffs went thru COVID period, and successfully passed two major milestones, CP and CZMA for two major projects (100MW), respectively. Confirmed 160MW aquafarm project.

Established Taiwan system organization and business in NSP, led NSP Asia system teams from scratch (2015Q4), and re-named as Taiwan System Business Unit in URE with >50 direct members later. This business unit expended to Project development, EPC, Sales, and O&M.

As a leader of Taiwan System Business Unit in URE, my team already sold more than 150 MW projects within 3 years, and developed pipeline more than 500MW, also executed operation and maintenance 100MW with 300 projects.

Together with finance team, we established energy fund (HsinZeTai, 新日泰) with with Cathay Life, which the largest finance group in Taiwan. Up to 3.5B NTD in initial fund phase, which allow to build 200MW system projects in Taiwan. We developed solar system projects, mainly rooftop type,to HsinZeTai 新日泰 in the early stage. That helps it met break even after two years.

Successfully merger and integrations five times in NSP and URE period. NSP meagered with Delsolar in 2015, and URE meagered from 3 IPOed companies (NSP, Gointech, Solartech) in 2018. I conducted effectively integrated Solartech system team in Oct. 2018, GES team in April 2019, and UREE EPC team in Jan. 2020. While integrations, I reconstructed and optimized organizations, and 60 direct staffs in this organization.

In Solar Cell and Modules Business:

Joined Ferro EMS and served to solar industry in the Greater China since 2002, I provided thick film technology to solar cell makers. Many of them become leading companies, such as Motech, E-ton, Suntech, Yingli, Csun and JA.

I received NSP founding employee award in 2010. In 2005 Sept, founded New Solar Power (NSP) as one of founders, then converted to first group of employees. Created sales organization and developed business in dynamically changed solar market. Dedicated a decade with team works, NSP grows to > 3,700 employees, 2.2GW in 2015.

NSP listed on the Taiwan Stock Exchange in Jan. 2009,and grown to tier 1 solar cell merchant company of the world after May 2013.

NSP sold 7.1GW solar cells until 2015, which owns global market share 3.5%.

In June 2015, I lead a worldwide sales and marketing (WSM) team with > 40 staffs. My team covers >75% NSP shipment in 2015.

The best revenue of NSP reaches USD 869M in 2014. It is continuously positive annual growth for 9 years in shipment qty.

In Electronic Material Industry:

Developed high performance multilayer capacitor. In EMEC UK project, I built a record high X7R capacitor with dielectric constant 14,000, which was higher than commercial type about 3 times.

I built Ceratron multilayer capacitor lab individually in 1997, and signed technology transfer agreement with Hitachi Japan.

Founded HolyStone ceramic capacitor lab and RD team started from scratch, and helped the firm transferred from a component trader to multilayer capacitor manufacturer.

With added manufacturing roles, HolyStone successfully went to OTC, and then IPO. Its stock price hit 999 (2000, 4, 12), which was highest in Taiwan in the year.

Received 2001 Elite youth Award in Taiwan ceramic society.
